The community

Discussion, attached to the work

The conversation lives where the content lives. Every episode, practice, and post carries its own discussion, so comments stay attached to the teaching that sparked them. Members reply to each other on the site, or jump straight to the video to join the public conversation.

Where comments live, three connected ways:
  1. On-site, under each piece of content. Every episode, Insight Timer practice, and Substack post has its own thread (native WordPress comments or the community plugin), so discussion sits right beneath the content.
  2. On the video itself. Each episode links out with a "Watch and comment on YouTube" button, so the public video conversation happens where the video lives.
  3. In themed spaces. For conversation beyond any single episode: introductions, rest and grief, the land.
